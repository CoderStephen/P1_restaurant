about

We are a fish restaurant based in the heart of galway. Our aim has always been to deliver high quality dishes using local Artisan, farmers and fisheries produce to support our local economy. We only use the freshest produce possible, our fish is caught daily and our veg is pesticide free. We support the local farmers and fish markets and change our restaurants menu seasonly. We provide elegant food but in a calm casual environment.

Caviar also supports young aspiring chefs. We work together with Galway ATU to aid in the training of beginner chefs. Every year we take on students and offer them a paid internship to help support them during their college training.
