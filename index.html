about

We are a fish restaurant based in the heart of galway. Our aim has always been to deliver high quality dishes using local Artisan, farmers and fisheries produce to support our local economy. We only use the freshest produce possible, our fish is caught daily and our veg is pesticide free. We support the local farmers and fish markets and our restaurants menu changes almost daily, depending on the freshest catch. We provide elegant food but in a calm casual environment.

our team

Our team here at Caviar have a deep passion and understanding of food. Our chefs are constantly experimenting in the kitchen to create new and exciting dishes for you to enjoy. Caviar also supports young aspiring chefs and food servers. We work together with Galway ATU to aid in the training of beginner chefs and food servers. Every year we take on students and offer them a paid internship to help support them during their college training. We feel this both helps us get well trained staff and also helps further their training and careers with no added financial stress.
